About Sodexo

Improving quality of life isn't just a dream. For Sodexo it's a mission. We believe quality of life helps to keep people healthy, happy & motivated throughout life. We believe it helps organisations to be more efficient and productive. And as the world leader of over 100 services, we believe that quality of life is created when we integrate our Food services, Facilities management, employee benefits and more But we go even further. Doing good business in a good way is our driver and at the heart of our mission. And our ambition is to positively impact one billion consumers worldwide.
