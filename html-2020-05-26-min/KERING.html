                                                        How Kering and its Houses are helping in the fight against the pandemic

                                                        Since January, Kering and its Houses have carried out a growing number of initiatives to help in the fight against the Covid-19 pandemic, supporting medical staff and health institutions, along with those affected by the virus. Given the scale of the situation, the Group has been active in several regions, each time adapting its support to the local needs.

                                                        Postponement of the Annual General Meeting to June 16, 2020

                                                        The Board of Directors, which met on April 21, 2020, decided that, based on current calendar constraints, the final date of the Annual Meeting of Shareholders will be June 16, 2020, at 3pm Paris time. Exceptionally, the Annual Meeting will be held behind closed doors.
